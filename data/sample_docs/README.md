# Renovex Hausmodernisierung GmbH — Customer Information & Service Guide

Renovex Hausmodernisierung GmbH specializes in residential renovation and interior modernization projects across the Saarland region. This knowledge base is used by the **Renovex Chat Assistant** to answer customer questions regarding services, pricing ranges, service areas, scheduling, and process steps.

---

## Services

- **Bathroom Renovation**
  - Tiling, waterproofing, shower/tub replacement, sanitary fixtures, ventilation
- **Kitchen Modernization**
  - Cabinet installation, countertop fitting, electrical/plumbing adjustments, backsplash tiling
- **Flooring Installation**
  - Laminate, vinyl, engineered wood, tile; subfloor prep as needed
- **Painting & Drywall**
  - Interior wall/ceiling painting, drywall repair & installation, finishing
- **Window & Door Replacement**
  - Frame adjustments, sealing, insulation and finishing trim
- **Basement Finishing**
  - Framing, insulation, drywall, lighting, flooring
- **Small Repairs / Handyman Services**
  - Fixture replacements, caulking, minor plumbing/electrical (where permitted)
- **Exterior Maintenance (light)**
  - Decking, fencing, gutter repair, siding spot fixes (season & weather dependent)

## Scheduling & Process

1. Customer describes project + shares photos (if possible)
2. Free **site visit** (core region) or virtual consultation
3. Itemized quote delivered within **2–5 business days**
4. Project scheduled after **20% deposit**
5. Work performed, updates communicated
6. Final walkthrough + workmanship warranty documentation
